About Us


Welcome to God Is Love Ministries - a place to grow in faith, build a community, and make an impact in the world.


Our Mission

Our mission is to share the love of God through worship, teaching, fellowship, and service. We seek to build a community where individuals are empowered to grow in their faith, experience the joy of serving others, and make a positive impact in our township.

We are committed to:

  • Worship: Honoring God through meaningful worship services.
  • Teaching: Helping people grow in their relationship with Christ through biblical teaching.
  • Fellowship: Building relationships and supporting each other in our walk with Christ.
  • Service: Reaching out to those in need, both locally and globally.
